Woodlands ActiveSG Gym

About

Woodlands ActiveSG Gym was previously known as Woodlands ClubFITT Gym and is operated by Sport Singapore.
It is conveniently located in the neighbourhood, serving its community with its well managed and upgraded facilities.
Looking forward, the gym is able to tap on the Woodlands Regional Centre for further growth and expansion.

Programmes

Programme Day/Time Description
Basic Strength & Conditioning Workout Every Thu/ 9.00am - 10.00am Addresses knee joint issues and high blood pressure problems through cardiovascular and strength and conditioning training.
Functional Fitness Training Every Sat/ 9.00am - 10.00am An effective fun-filled weight-loss focused functional training programme which allows you to work on multiple muscle groups. Suitable for all ages.
Circuit Training Every Sun/ 9.30am - 10.30am Get into basic fitness condition or challenge yourself to out-do the records, the regime is for all fitness levels. Suitable for all ages.
